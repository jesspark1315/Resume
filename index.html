✦ Product Philosophy
How I Build Products
01
Build systems, not features
Features solve one problem once. Systems create compounding returns. I design for the feedback loop, not the launch moment.
02
Turn intent into measurable outcomes
Strategy without instrumentation is just hope. I instrument behavior change from day one and let data force prioritization decisions.
03
AI as a decision layer, not a feature
The best AI products don't feel like AI products. They feel like the system got smarter. I build toward that — quality and trust before capability.
04
Design for behavior change, not usability
Usability gets you adoption. Behavior change gets you retention. The question is never "can users do this?" — it's "will they?"

Customer Agent AI
0 to 1M+ Resolutions.

CompanyHubSpot
RoleSenior PM, AI Agents
TimelineDec '22 – Jul '25

The story of how I took HubSpot's first AI support agent from a failed public beta to one of its fastest-growing products — by betting on quality and trust over features and speed.

✦ As presented at HubSpot Inbound '24
▶
Watch on YouTube — HubSpot Inbound '24
01
The Problem
HubSpot was behind — and the moment demanded a smarter approach.
Support volume soared post-COVID. LLMs reshaped customer expectations overnight. Rules-based bots couldn't keep up and HubSpot had underinvested. With 200K+ customers already on the platform, we had the data and reach to win — but we had to move fast.
→ lean team. 2 months to MVP.
02
The Failure
We launched before we were ready. 60% negative CSAT.
Leadership committed to a spring marketing launch. My instincts said we weren't ready — quality wasn't there and we hadn't built trust. We shipped anyway. ~100 activated users. 15% resolution rate. 60% negative CSAT. The data confirmed what I already knew.

→ "trust my instincts. don't rush to beta under pressure."
03
The Reset
Full product reset. Rebuilt trust. Rebranded from chatbot to agent.
I led a complete reset — not just the roadmap but the team structure, the product narrative, and the GTM strategy. Ruthlessly prioritized quality over features. Re-architected the RAG pipeline. Reframed the product from "AI chatbot" to "Customer Agent." Unveiled the rebrand at Inbound '24.
→ "it works" became "it works reliably."
04
The Outcome
5× WAU. 1M+ resolutions. One of HubSpot's fastest-growing AI products.
Five months after the reset launch: 5× weekly active users, 35%+ resolution rate, 3,500+ paying customers with 50%+ active weekly. Scaled to 1M+ total resolutions. The product became the blueprint for HubSpot's broader AI strategy.

✦ What I'd do again
01Trust my instincts. Don't rush to beta under pressure from leadership or timelines.
02Quality is the key to trust. Easy onboarding won't drive AI adoption — reliable answers will.
03Shipping is better than not shipping — but only if you've built the feedback loops to learn fast.
04AI trust is fragile. It's faster to lose than to gain. Design for trust from day one.
